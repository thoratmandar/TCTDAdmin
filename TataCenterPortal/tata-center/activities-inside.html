Swadesh Foundation

Previous Next

Various field visits have been undertaken in the villages of Konkan Region, Maharashtra. Apart from learning as the prime objective of the field visits,possibilities of collaborative work for technological intervention in rural communities was also explored. The field visits were facilitated by Swades Foundation which works on various local issues and rural empowerment.

Muniseva Ashram, Gujrat

Previous Next

Tata Centre for Technology and Design visited the Muni Seva Ashram premises located at Goraj village in Vadodara, Gujarat. Hospitals were visited to understand their daily functions using electronic systems, radiation therapy for cancer treatment, medical waste treatment plant using plasma pyrolysis. Their agricultural facilities, old age homes, children homes etc. were also visited. Tata Centre’s team was able to understand ground realities with very fine detail of the Ashram’s activities, facilities, wide varieties of challenges etc. These understandings will help the team to work on many areas where technology can could play a pivotal role in improving the lives of the people.
