08.06.2017

Open House 2017 at Tata Centre

It was a pleasant pre-monsoon Monday morning, but unlike most days, this was an illustrious day for the 16 young Tata Fellows from the 2015-17 batch who presented their efforts as researchers for two years.

This Open House event meant to exhibit their work through posters, prototypes and other media kicked off, as scheduled. The fellows worked hard with their respective posters, anxiously voicing their opinion about their research work. There was a wide spectrum of audience that attended the event, ranging from undergraduates to postdoctoral fellows, departmental heads to deans of the academic community, representatives from centres within IITB, the industry, the US Consulate, SEARCH Foundation, and some interested consultants, to name a few.

This year marked the second annual poster presentation. Each fellow was also made to record a 2-3 minutes’ video, to be uploaded soon on the TCTD website. At the end of the day, the Fellows pulled off a great show.
